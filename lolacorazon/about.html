About Us

Our Story

On the plain overlooking Mount Arayat, Lola Corazon Leisure Farmhas a state-of-the-art Pavilion having a complete sound system and can accommodate up to 300 people. It is adjacent to a garden surrounded with flora and fauna and large swimming pools for both kids and adults. Beyond the garden is a lovely rest house set in the middle of a pond which offers a soothing view of ornamental fish. Native inspired room accommodations are also available for people staying overnight. Huts of bamboo, nipa, sawali and anahaw are spread over the place that can cater up to 150 people.
